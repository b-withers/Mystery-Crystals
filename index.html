Mystery Crystal Game

You will be given a random Goal to start the game. Each of the four Mystery Crystals below will be assigned a random hidden number. By clicking on the Mystery Crystals you will add the hidden amount to your Current Total. You win the game by matching your Current Total to the Goal, you lose by going over! Each time the game restarts, you are given a new Goal, and the Mystery Crystals hidden amounts are reset.
