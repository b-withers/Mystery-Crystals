Mystery Crystal Game

You will be Given a random number to start the game. Each of the four mystery crystals below will add a specific amount to your total score. You win the game by matching your total to the random number, you lose by going over! Each time the game restarts, you are given a new random number, and the mystery crystals hidden amounts are reset.
